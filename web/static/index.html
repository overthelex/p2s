// Protocol

How it works

Services publish self-describing cards to a distributed hash table. Agents discover them in seconds without crawling.

> Identity

Your key is your identity

No URLs, no usernames, no registrars. An Ed25519 keypair IS the service. The DHT address is BLAKE3(pubkey). Change your endpoint without changing your identity.

> Verification

Cards are self-verifying

Every card is signed by the publisher's private key. Any node can verify it against the address. No node is trusted — a tampered card won't verify.

> Discovery

Push, not crawl

Publishing a card replicates it to responsible nodes immediately via Kademlia DHT. Consumer agents see new cards within seconds, not hours.

> Naming

Custom TLDs

Register .vovkes, .100500, or any name outside ICANN. Commit-reveal protocol prevents front-running. Deterministic tiebreaker — no arbiters.
